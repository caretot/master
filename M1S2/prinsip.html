Memory tip

8 OGR + 6 POGR = 14 region "families". Your exam groups Kurdamir-Saatli and Saatli-Lankaran into ONE lecture (11), but some textbooks (Yusifov & Aslanov, industry reports) count them as TWO separate POGRs. If the exam asks "how many possible oil-gas regions are there", the textbook answer is 6: Ajinohur, Kurdamir-Saatli, Saatli-Lankaran, Gazakh-Dallar-Dalimammadli, Transtalysh(Jalilabad), Nakhchivan.
